About Hobby

I started fishing when I was about 6 years old. I used to go fishing all the time with my dad. Once I got old enough to go to boy scouts, I had leaders that taught me how to fly fish. All throughout Jr. High and Highschool, I went backpacking and fishing during the summer months. I learned to love the outdoors and fishing and it has grown to be one of my biggest hobbies. Now, any free time I have, I spend knee-deep in a river fly fishing. It helps me be at peace and connect with nature.

Most hobbies involve choosing your favorite pieces of equipment or whatever is used for the hobby. Some of my favorite equipment with reguards to fly fishing are:

  • Felt Bottom Boots
  • Chest-High Waders
  • Dry Flies
    1. Catis
    2. Parachute Adams
    3. Foam Hoppers
  • Wet Flies
    1. Bead-Head Nymphs
    2. Stone Flies
  • Fishing Vest
  • Polarized Sunglasses
  • Lucky Fishing Hat
